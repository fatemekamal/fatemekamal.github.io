About Me

I'm a UX/UI designer who blends user research, usability testing, and iterative design to create digital experiences that are clear, accessible, and delightful. I believe great design starts with understanding people — their goals, frustrations, and the contexts in which they interact with technology.

My work spans healthcare platforms, wedding marketplaces, and e-commerce — from early discovery research and information architecture all the way through high-fidelity prototypes and usability validation.

I thrive in collaborative, cross-functional teams and enjoy turning complex problems into intuitive, evidence-based solutions.

UX/UI Case Study

Dr. Pal

A Responsive Healthcare Platform

Redesigned the responsive website for Dr. Pal, an AI-powered healthcare platform that provides intelligent tools for both doctors and patients — including an automatic SOAP note generator for clinicians and a multilingual chatbot for patient symptom checks.

Challenge

The previous site didn't clearly communicate what tools the platform offered. Users were confused by the information architecture, vague product descriptions, and lack of trust-building elements.

Approach

Heuristic evaluation, competitive analysis, persona development, storyboarding, site map restructuring, iterative wireframing, A/B testing, and multi-round usability testing across mobile and desktop.

Outcome

Delivered a clear, accessible responsive design with improved navigation, transparent product descriptions, and step-by-step onboarding — validated through usability testing with both doctor and patient user groups.

UX/UI Case Study

DreamWed

Wedding Vendor Website Design

Designed a B2C platform connecting couples with trusted wedding vendors across the U.S. Vendors create accounts to list services; couples search, filter, and request meetings — all within a streamlined experience.

Challenge

Design the site's information architecture from scratch and create an intuitive venue selection flow — each vendor category has a unique research process that needed to be accommodated.

Approach

Survey (18 participants), competitive analysis, user interviews, secondary research, affinity diagramming, persona creation, Double Diamond methodology, paper sketches through high-fidelity prototyping, and iterative usability testing.

Outcome

Delivered a fully structured platform with user-validated information architecture, optimized search and filter flows, and a design system inspired by nature's softness and elegance.

UX Case Study

Mikhak Talaee

Flower Shop Web Redesign

Redesigned the information architecture and flower purchasing experience for Mikhak Talaee, an Iranian online flower shop offering flowers, indoor plants, and gift delivery for special occasions across Tehran.

Challenge

Improve customer retention and sales by simplifying navigation and streamlining the ordering process for users ordering from different cities for Tehran delivery.

Approach

Survey (47 participants), competitive analysis, interviews and contextual inquiry (25 users), affinity diagramming, user journey mapping, card sorting (open + hybrid), iterative wireframing, and usability testing.

Outcome

Restructured the site based on user mental models via card sorting, introduced occasion-based filters as primary categories, and validated the redesigned purchasing flow through usability testing.
